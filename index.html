About Me

I'm a results-driven Aerospace V&V Expert and Program Manager with over 16 years delivering safety-critical software in compliance with DO-178B/C (DAL-A through DAL-D) across Rolls-Royce, Airbus and Boeing programs. My depth spans system-level verification and validation, DO-178C planning documentation (PSAC, SVP, SQAP, SCMP) and cross-functional technical leadership at SAFRAN, AMETEK, TATA and Aerotek.

I complement that aerospace foundation with hands-on product ownership in embedded IoT and connected-device startups. Currently at Frost Active, I own the full product lifecycle — from POC and certification through GTM and beta launch.
